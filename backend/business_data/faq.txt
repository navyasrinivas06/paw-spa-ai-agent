Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Do you groom all breeds?
A: Yes. We provide professional grooming services for dogs and cats of all breeds and sizes.

Q: Do you accept walk-ins?
A: Yes, subject to availability. However, we recommend booking an appointment in advance to avoid waiting.

Q: Are appointments required?
A: Appointments are recommended to ensure your preferred date and time are available.

Q: Do you provide pet boarding?
A: Yes. We provide safe, clean, comfortable, and supervised boarding facilities for pets.

Q: Do you groom puppies?
A: Yes. We provide gentle and safe grooming services specially designed for puppies.

Q: What payment methods do you accept?
A: We accept Cash, UPI, Google Pay, PhonePe, Paytm, and Bank Transfer.

Q: Do you provide pickup and drop services?
A: Yes. Pickup and drop services are available at ₹24.50 per KM.

Q: How long does grooming take?
A: Grooming usually takes between 1 and 3 hours depending on the pet's breed, size, coat condition, and selected services.

Q: Should my pet be vaccinated before boarding?
A: Yes. We recommend that all boarding pets are up to date with their vaccinations to ensure the safety of every pet.

Q: Can I bring my pet's food and medications?
A: Yes. Please provide sufficient food, medications, and clear instructions during check-in.

Q: Do you handle aggressive pets?
A: Please contact us before booking. Our team will evaluate each case to ensure the safety of both pets and staff.
