About Us

Paw Spa & Nest is a professional pet grooming and boarding center located in Hyderabad.

Our mission is to provide a clean, safe, comfortable, and stress-free experience for every pet. We treat every pet like a member of our own family and provide personalized care through trained professionals.

We specialize in grooming, spa treatments, boarding, hygiene care, and pet wellness services for both dogs and cats.
